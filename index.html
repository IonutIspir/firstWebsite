What our place offers

A quiet place

The wood cabin is located in a remote, quiet place, perfect for nature lovers and for those who want to get away from the daily life. We have no electricity but we have light provided by a car battery. We have no running water, no bathroom, but we have an exterior toilet and a heated shower, so you can feel closer to nature.

A great location

Beautifully decorated and featuring a sweeping staircase, original stained-glass windows, period furniture, and a stunningly unique black-and-white tiled bathroom. 100% of recent guests gave the location a 5-star rating.

Pet friendly stay

Pets are allowed with no extra payment.
